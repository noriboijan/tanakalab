QoE (Quality of Experience)

Recent years, in development of fixed and mobile network infrastructure, we can receive benefit from the service regardless of time or location. Accordingly, not only services are diversified but also users' location, purpose, and behavior are diversified. Previous services are evaluated and controlled by QoS (Quality of Service) which evaluates by using packet loss rate, delay, and so on. However, qualities is different to experience depending on the person even if providers give the same QoS. QoE (Quality of Experience) ,that evaluates based on a quality is subjectively recognized by each user, has attracted research attentions. In our laboratory, we study that users' QoE is affected by realistic environmental and psychological factors and we design a network system to control based on QoE for comfort and convenient.
